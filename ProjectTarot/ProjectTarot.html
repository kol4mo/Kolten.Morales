Project Tarot is the first game I made in college with help Chelsea Bui in December of 2022 . This game is a sidescroller shoot em up that you play as a magical witch corgi. you shoot fireballs using space and move left and right using a and d or left and right. you can also choose different powers in the main menu through Tarot. changing that will change your attack style.
