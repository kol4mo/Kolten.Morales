Desert Dash

Play Desert Dash on Itch.io

Desert Dash is a high-speed multiplayer racing game developed for the Spring 2024 Neumont Game Jam, created in collaboration with Makenzie Buchenroth. Featuring fast-paced gameplay and a unique Moebius-inspired shader aesthetic, Desert Dash lets players race across sun-scorched terrain in competitive chaos. Built with Unity and designed for both local and online multiplayer.
