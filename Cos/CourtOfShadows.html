Court of Shadows was developed during the August 2023 Neumont Game Jam in collaboration with Makenzie Buchenroth. I focused on gameplay scripting, dialogue system logic, and narrative structuring to support the theme "You Are the Villain." My work involved writing conditional decision trees, implementing branching paths, and integrating UI elements that reflect the player's moral choices. I also contributed to balancing tone and pacing to ensure the politically themed narrative felt grounded, impactful, and replayable.
